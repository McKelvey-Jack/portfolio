About me

Hello there and welcome to my portfolio page

This is normally you might find someone talking about how they have dreamed of becoming a software developer since they were old enough to walk. I won't lie to you, that’s not true in my case. I made it to 25 without so much as giving a second thought to how software was built. I was too busy trying to learn japanese… After my degree I began working as an IT recruitment consultant in Manchester. When the coronavirus pandemic hit I thought I would learn to code when I was placed on furlough from my recruitment role. At first it was to help me improve in that job but I quickly realised that rather than chase developers around, I wanted to be one! Fast forward a few months I am coming to the end of the brilliant coding bootcamp at northcoders. So far I have loved every second of it. You can view some of my work both from the bootcamp, and my weekends further down this page. Jack
